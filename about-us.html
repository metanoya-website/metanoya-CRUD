About Us

Know About Us

In India, there is a prevailing ignorance of help-based education and home-schooling for children with specific learning differences.
The resources for the same are too scarce and scattered as compared to the prevalence and geographic distribution of SLDs.

METANOYA creates diverse education models coupled with an interactive learning environment that is not confined to a one-size-fits-all classroom. Instead, we strive to provide a user-friendly unified approach which help children with specific learning differences and others learn together at the same pace in a symbiotic environment.
We as a group of young entrepreneurs cherish the thought of developing entrepreneurial models imposing deep social impact leading to enhanced social well-being and quality of life.
With the initiative of METANOYA we delve deeper into skills of intertwining a business model into social impact to uplift lives of individuals with learning differences.
