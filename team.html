Meet Our Team

Team

A dedicated group of mentors at METANOYA who is trained to identify individuals with SLDs and co-occurrences is well equipped with sound research-based intervention to help our students achieve their future educational and social pursuits effectively.
They are qualified counsellors, psychologists, Montessori teachers, special educators, story tellers, social workers and many others with variety of different skillsets who make METANOYA inclusive and holistic.
Proudly we call them 'META-MENTORS'!
