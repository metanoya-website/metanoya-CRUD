From Founder's Desk

I extol the Beauty which lies in diversity. That's how we are built to perceive this world. That's the thing which mesmerises a child every moment since birth. Nature is perfect in nurturing such variegation lying in and around us.

Neurotypicals have a tendency to modify the meaning of words like intelligence, success, health, happiness, need and progress for rest of the society via massive apologia, yet the reflection of efforts like METANOYA invigorates and preserves the milieu of Neurodiversity by help construct a niche of respect, tolerance, integrity, inclusion and competitive advantage for a neurodivergent individual by replacing isolation and stigma from the education world. We made this achievable by building an exclusive learning platform to start with. I believe that a perfect blend of home-schooling, parental training, curated interventions, and guided pedagogy can do wonders for kids with learning differences. I hope this start-up will bring in a social insight and the wind of change within the conventional education system towards an inclusive learning environment – Dr. Vivek P.
