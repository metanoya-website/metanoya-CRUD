METANOYA

Metanoya

METANOYA is a unified learning platform dedicated to children of different age groups with specific learning differences.
METANOYA will achieve learning-based goals with a multimodal approach towards awareness, detection, remediation and an array of psychological counselling.

About Us

The methods of teaching will be implemented in conjunction with formal education as well as special organisations; thus, creating an inclusive learning atmosphere that will be easily accessible and will help achieve students' career-oriented goals eventually.

Know More

Complications

At METANOYA we focus on a change in the educational system from traditional classroom teaching methods to inclusive practices that relies on multi-sensory and need-based pedagogy affirming Universal Design for Learning guidelines.

Outstanding

A graded curriculum is specifically curated to help out all our students and affiliate schools.

Social problems

Each of our 'Meta-mentors' will be passionate about helping both parents and students keeping an empathic and scientific approach towards them.

Problems as adults

The awareness programs, screening and dynamic assessments for SLDs, literacy and psycho-behavioural analysis remain important components of our product and service. Both online & offline teaching modules will be provided in the form of technology-based hybrid solution to every doorstep.
