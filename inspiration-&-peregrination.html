Inspiration & Peregrination

Inspiration & Peregrination

An example of great entrepreneurial capabilities of dyslexics and life events of struggle and success of a dear friend evoked the thought that there is a dire need of a social support system and tools of specific help for learning disabilities at doorstep level.
Mr. Adwait Vikram Singh faced the austere reality of having developmental Dyslexia at a very early age.
Incidents of discrimination, bullying and isolation dawned out of it amid a best formal school setup until awareness and sacrifice of a dedicated mother and early intervention made him go all the way up to Harvard Business school for education and turned him into a successful and inspiring business person.
Multiple encounters with such agonising but still inspiring life stories and the lame social support in India affirmed on us to initiate our efforts towards change through METANOYA.
