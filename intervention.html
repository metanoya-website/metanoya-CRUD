Intervention

A wide array of research-based interventions (e.g. IEPs, psychotherapy, CBT, counselling) are designed to help candidates with learning differences at academic, social and psychological levels to achieve occupational and social well-being.Implementation of specifically curated study material at early age, training parents with techniques of special education for home schooling dyslexic kids can bring profound improvement, enabling them to become part of mainstream formal education system.

We have designed a unique research based hybrid learning platform and our protocols has been adapted to regional and sociocultural variables which makes METANOYA amenable to world wide acceptance and deeper penetration amongst masses.
